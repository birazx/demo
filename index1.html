Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo born on february 5 1985 AD is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward for and captains both Saudi Pro League club Al Nassr and the Portugal national team. He is the greatest football player of all time.

footballing career

Cristiano started his footballing career from sporting lisbon. later he joined manchester united in 2003 and achieved numerous titles and awards. he is still the last player to win golden ball from premier league. In 2009, he joined the spanish side real madrid on the record transfer of $90 million. during his campaign in madrid he became the most complete player ever. he won 4 champions league with real madrid as well as 4 golden balls. overall, he has had a spectacular career.
